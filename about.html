Whistle Pig Wood Shop

Most of my stuff is made with a CNC machine- I send digital art into a computer-controlled router. Even though part of my process is machine made, most of it is spent cutting, designing, sanding, and assembling the pieces by hand.

Almost all of my projects are made out of a combination of domestic and exotic hardwoods. I prefer to avoid stain, and instead, let the natural figure and colors of the wood shine through.

I only do custom orders, so feel free to send me a message if you would like something unique!

About me

I'm a software developer living in Blacksburg VA. Everything I own is dusty and covered in beard hair. Aside from making wood art in my spare time, I take naps. In between wood art and naps, I like to make websites and games.
